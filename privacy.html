Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

MakeMeasure ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MakeMeasure platform and related services (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. If you join a workspace, we collect your role and workspace membership details.

Workspace Content. We store content you and your team create within the platform, including text, images, files, and project data. This content belongs to you.

Connected Advertising Account Data. If you choose to connect third-party advertising accounts (such as Meta Ads, Google Ads, or TikTok Ads), we access data from those platforms on your behalf via their official APIs. This includes:

  • Account information: Ad account name, account ID, and account status.
  • Campaign structure: Campaign, ad set, and ad identifiers and names.
  • Performance metrics: Impressions, reach, clicks, spend, cost-per-result, conversions, and related delivery and engagement metrics.

We access this data in read-only mode. We do not modify your advertising accounts, campaigns, or ad creative unless you explicitly initiate an action within the Service. We do not collect payment information, billing details, or audience list data from connected advertising platforms.

Workspace Activity Data. As you use the Service, we collect structured records of your workspace activity, including project decisions, content revisions, production timelines, and how your campaigns perform over time. We use this data to improve the intelligence and relevance of the Service for your workspace. This data is stored within your workspace and is not shared with other users in identifiable form.

Usage Data. We collect standard usage information such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and feature usage patterns to improve the Service.

Cookies. We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Display advertising performance data you have authorised us to access
  • Analyse your workspace's historical activity to provide more relevant and context-aware recommendations over time
  • Generate anonymised, aggregated benchmarks across the platform to help all users understand broader performance patterns (see Aggregated Benchmarks below)
  • Communicate with you about your account and the Service
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

We do not use your content or connected account data to train machine learning models. Your data is yours.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information or advertising account data to third parties. We share information only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers. We use third-party services to operate the platform, including cloud hosting (Vercel, Supabase), authentication, and data storage. These providers access your data only as necessary to perform their services and are bound by contractual obligations to protect it.

AI Processing. Portions of your workspace content and historical workspace activity may be processed by third-party AI services (Anthropic) to provide intelligent features within the platform. The AI provider does not retain your data beyond the duration of each request. However, MakeMeasure stores structured summaries derived from your workspace activity and uses these summaries as context when generating recommendations for your workspace. This means the Service becomes more relevant to your team's specific patterns over time. These summaries are workspace-specific and are not shared with other users.

Aggregated Benchmarks. We may use anonymised, aggregated data from across the platform to generate performance benchmarks and pattern insights. This data is stripped of any identifying information before aggregation. No individual workspace's campaigns, content, or strategies are visible to other users. You may opt out of contributing to aggregated benchmarks in your workspace settings at any time without affecting your use of the Service.

Advertising Platforms. When you connect an advertising account, we communicate with that platform's API to retrieve your performance data. We do not share your data with these platforms beyond what is necessary to authenticate your connection. Our use of data received from advertising platform APIs adheres to each platform's applicable developer terms and data use policies, including the Meta Platform Terms, Google API Services User Data Policy, and TikTok Marketing API Terms of Service.

Legal Requirements. We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase, with data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). Connected advertising account data is stored in the same infrastructure and subject to the same encryption and access controls. We enforce row-level security policies so that workspace data is accessible only to authenticated members of that workspace. We conduct regular security reviews and follow industry-standard practices for access management and credential handling.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and workspace data for as long as your account is active.

Connected advertising account data is retained for as long as the relevant advertising account remains connected to the Service. If you disconnect an advertising account, we delete the associated performance data within 30 days. If you delete your MakeMeasure account, all connected advertising account data is deleted within the same 30-day period.

Account and workspace data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion, except where retention is required by law or to resolve disputes.

We do not retain advertising account data after it is no longer necessary to provide the Service.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access and export your data at any time
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account settings
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us at hello@makemeasure.com
  • Disconnect any linked advertising account at any time through your workspace settings, which immediately revokes our access to that platform's data and triggers deletion of the associated data within 30 days
  • Request deletion of specific advertising account data without deleting your full account by contacting us at hello@makemeasure.com
  • Opt out of contributing to aggregated platform benchmarks in your workspace settings

As a Canadian company, we comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, request corrections, withdraw consent for non-essential uses, and request deletion. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@makemeasure.com.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion.

7. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.
